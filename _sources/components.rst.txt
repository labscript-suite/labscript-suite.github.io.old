
*labscript suite* components
============================

The *labscript suite* is modular by design, and is comprised of:

.. list-table:: Python libraries
    :widths: 10 90
    :header-rows: 0

    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/labscript_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/labscript/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |labscript|_ --- Expressive composition of hardware-timed experiments
    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/labscript_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/labscript-devices/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |labscript-devices|_ --- Plugin architecture for controlling experiment hardware
    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/labscript_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/labscript-utils/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |labscript-utils|_ --- Shared modules used by the *labscript suite*
    

.. list-table:: Graphical applications
    :widths: 10 90
    :header-rows: 0

    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/runmanager_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/runmanager/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |runmanager|_ --- Graphical and remote interface to parameterized experiments
    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/blacs_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/blacs/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |blacs|_ --- Graphical interface to scientific instruments and experiment supervision
    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/lyse_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/lyse/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |lyse|_ --- Online analysis of live experiment data
    
    * - .. image:: ../../art/runviewer_32nx32n.svg
             :target: https://docs.labscriptsuite.org/projects/runviewer/en/latest/
             :class: labscript-suite-icon
      - |runviewer|_ --- Visualize hardware-timed experiment instructions
